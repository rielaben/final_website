Michigan Sports Analytics Society

We are a student-led group at the University of Michigan working to cultivate interest in the field of sports analytics through discussion, student engagement, and various project opportunities.

About

Statistics and data analysis are the future of sports. More so than ever before, teams in every sport use data to make personnel decisions and devise winning strategies. Learn about how teams do this analysis, find peers for collaboration on research projects, and build your resume with skills attractive to franchises, agencies, and other sports organizations.

Our Goals

MSAS is established for the expressed purpose of assisting members of the University of Michigan community in increasing their experience, knowledge, and skill in using statistics and analytical techniques to analyze athletic competition. The mission of the Michigan Sport Analytics Society is to learn and collaborate on projects that can help our members develop analytical tools that will help them solve issues in the world of sport.

Who Are We?

The Michigan Sports Analytics Society is a student-led group at the University of Michigan working to cultivate interest in the field of sports analytics through discussion, student engagement, and various project opportunities.
